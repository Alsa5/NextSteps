1. BUSINESS CONTEXT & PROBLEM STATEMENT
================================================================================

What Hexaware Is Solving
------------------------
Hexaware recruits college students through campus placements and interviews.
These recruits are NOT immediately onboarded as employees. They enter a structured
Maverick training program first.

  GET  — Graduate Engineer Trainee (4 LPA band)
  PGET — Premier Graduate Engineer Trainee (6 LPA band)

Both are commonly called Mavericks.

Training Outcomes
-----------------
During training, Mavericks may experience:

  Outcome            | Meaning
  -------------------|--------------------------------------------------------
  Batch segregation  | AI/manual grouping into training streams by skills,
                     | resume, certificates
  Batch separation   | Splitting or reassigning cohorts mid-program
  Conversion         | Successfully completing training → formal onboarding
  Let-down           | Failing training → removal from program; Letter of
                     | Intent (LOI) may be revoked

Upon conversion/onboarding, the Maverick receives a Hexaware corporate email
(@hexaware.com) and transitions from pre-deployment to post-deployment identity.

Hackathon Sources (Merged Into One Product)
-------------------------------------------
A. Maverick Execution Platform
   End-to-end training operations: batch lifecycle, candidate data, attendance,
   assessments, trainer coordination, reminders, notifications, reporting,
   real-time dashboards, and performance insights. Replaces spreadsheets and
   manual coordination.

B. Maverick Feedback 360
   Automated feedback collection: post-session training feedback, post-project
   performance insights (~3 months after deployment), AI analytics, automated
   reminders, dashboards targeting 75–80% feedback completion, training
   effectiveness tracking, and performance alerts for L&D.

NextSteps combines both: training execution + feedback + deployment continuity +
talent insights.

Platform Narrative
------------------
We started with Maverick Feedback 360 — a feedback system.
Then we realised: feedback without context is just data.
Context comes from knowing who the Maverick is → Resume Intelligence.
Context comes from knowing how the session went → Transcript Intelligence.
Context comes from knowing how they're placed → Batch Segregation AI.
And feedback is worthless if nobody submits it → Gamification fixes that.

So Feedback 360 pulled in Execution and Talent — not because we went out of
scope, but because feedback demanded them. NextSteps is what happens when you
follow the problem all the way to its roots.

The result:
  - L&D makes data-driven decisions in minutes, not weeks
  - Trainers see their real impact — not just attendance numbers
  - Mavericks feel they are on a journey — not just surviving training
  - Hexaware deploys better, more prepared engineers. Every batch.

3. PRODUCT VISION
================================================================================

NextSteps is a unified, cloud-native, metaverse-inspired platform that manages
the entire Maverick journey:

  Recruitment acceptance → Welcome & orientation → Training (Phases 0–4) →
  Assessments → Feedback → Conversion OR Let-down → Onboarding → Deployment →
  Post-project performance review (M1, M3, M6)

Experience Principles
---------------------
  - Whimsical, story-driven UI — training feels like a mission/world, not a
    corporate form library
  - 60-30-10 color rule — dominant base (60%), secondary structure (30%),
    accent highlights (10%)
  - Immersive interactions — parallax scrolling, React Three Fiber (WebGL/3D),
    physics engines (e.g., Rapier/Cannon.js)
  - Identity through lanyard — React Bits lanyard component on login and in
    identity surfaces
  - Gamification — XP, badges, streaks, batch leaderboards, daily missions,
    level progression, skill tree
  - Privacy-first analytics — aggregate batch insights only; no individual
    surveillance
  - Dark-mode HUD for Mavericks; clean command center for L&D and Trainers

Strategic Outcomes
------------------
  Outcome                  | Success Signal
  -------------------------|--------------------------------------------------
  Higher engagement        | ↑ attendance, feedback completion, daily missions
  Faster trainer response  | Confusion alerts within one session cycle
  Smarter batch design     | AI segregation cuts manual grouping time ≥50%
  Seamless deployment      | Personal-email auth → Hexaware SSO, zero data loss
  Manager confidence       | Training passport + early performance flags
  Feedback completion      | 75–80% across pulse and deep feedback cycles
  Data-driven L&D          | Curriculum decisions in minutes, not weeks

5. AUTHENTICATION MODEL (CRITICAL)
================================================================================

AUTH REALITY
------------
  - Mavericks in Phases 0–4 have NO Hexaware ID. They join with personal Gmail.
  - Trainers are external vendors — they do NOT have Hexaware mail IDs.
  - L&D Executives and Managers are Hexaware employees with corporate email.

NextSteps uses a Dual/Triple Auth System:
  - Magic Link OTP for Mavericks (pre-deployment) and Trainers (vendor email)
  - Hexaware SSO (Azure AD) for L&D Executives, Managers, and deployed Mavericks

At Phase 5 deployment: Maverick links personal Gmail account to new Hexaware
email — seamless history transfer on same canonical userId.

5.1 Maverick (GET/PGET) — Dual-State Auth
-----------------------------------------
  Phase              | Auth Method              | Email Used
  -------------------|--------------------------|-----------------------------
  Pre-deployment     | Gmail Magic Link OTP     | Personal email (college/Gmail)
  (Phases 0–4)       | + optional 6-digit PIN   |
  Post-deployment    | Hexaware SSO (Azure AD)  | New @hexaware.com email
  (Phase 5+)         |                          |

Requirements:
  - Single canonical userId across both auth modes
  - Preserve XP, badges, feedback, skills, batch history, readiness score
  - Force one-time re-login at SSO cutover with explanatory banner
  - Lanyard re-renders with "Deployed" badge (visual only)
  - Track lifecycle states: invited → active_training → converted → deployed
    OR let_down
  - Internal NextSteps Profile ID ties all data (not Gmail, not Hex ID)
  - Tokens expire in 90 days; PIN reset via Gmail OTP only
  - All Maverick data encrypted at rest

Onboarding Token Flow (Maverick):
  1. L&D bulk-uploads Maverick Gmail list via CSV into Batch Composer
  2. NextSteps generates unique Onboarding Token per Maverick; sends Magic Link
     to Gmail
  3. Maverick clicks link → Onboarding Wizard → sets 6-digit PIN → activated
  4. All XP, badges, feedback, quiz scores tied to internal Profile ID
  5. Deployment: L&D clicks "Activate Hex ID" → verification link to new
     Hexaware email
  6. Maverick clicks link → accounts merged → SSO login from this point forward

9. SESSION TRANSCRIPT INTELLIGENCE
================================================================================

Design Philosophy
-----------------
All Maverick training sessions are online (Google Meet / Microsoft Teams).
Transcripts are captured but NOT used to monitor individual Mavericks. They
improve training content, clarity, and curriculum.

  Who          | What They See                          | Purpose
  -------------|----------------------------------------|-----------------------------
  Maverick     | Own session transcript. Searchable.    | Personal study aid
               | AI 5-bullet summary + 3 key terms.     |
  Trainer      | Aggregate confusion signals: timestamps| Improve delivery — WHEN
               | where clarity dipped + transcript      | and WHERE batch confused
               | context. NOT individual attributions.  |
  L&D Executive| Cross-session topic coverage analysis. | Curriculum improvement
               | Rushed topics, gaps, repetition.       |
  Manager      | NO access — internal to training phase |

Why This Is NOT Micromanagement
-------------------------------
  - Individual Maverick contributions NEVER surfaced to L&D or Trainers
  - Confusion signals are batch-level aggregates
  - Maverick transcript is self-study tool only
  - Transcription opt-in per session at L&D level — not always-on
  - Goal: curriculum intelligence, not individual performance policing

DESIGN PRINCIPLE: Transcripts make the TRAINING smarter, not the oversight heavier.

10. AI-POWERED BATCH SEGREGATION & RESUME INTELLIGENCE
================================================================================

The Problem It Solves
---------------------
  BEFORE: L&D manually reviews hundreds of resumes → guesses skill levels →
          assigns batches → discovers mismatches in Week 3.
  AFTER:  Maverick uploads resume + certs → AI parses in seconds → skill profile
          → batch grouping recommended → L&D approves in one click.

The Flow — Step by Step
-----------------------
  01. Maverick receives NextSteps Onboarding Token (Magic Link to Gmail)
  02. Onboarding Wizard Step 1: Upload Resume (PDF)
      Step 2: Upload certificates (AWS, Coursera, NPTEL, GitHub link, etc.)
  03. AI parses resume → programming languages, frameworks, projects,
      internships, academic scores, certifications
  04. NER identifies tech entities. ML scores proficiency per domain
  05. AI generates Maverick Skill Profile: skill tags, proficiency levels,
      domain affinities, stream fit scores
  06. L&D opens Batch Composer. AI recommends groupings with rationale
  07. L&D reviews/approves/adjusts in one screen → batches created, Mavericks
      notified, trainers assigned
  08. Skill Profile becomes baseline for all subsequent quiz/feedback measurement

What AI Extracts
----------------
  Source              | Data Extracted                    | Used For
  --------------------|-----------------------------------|---------------------------
  Resume PDF          | Languages, frameworks, projects,    | Base skill profile,
                      | internships, CGPA, achievements   | stream affinity
  Certificates        | Cert name, issuer, date, score      | Domain credibility,
                      |                                   | fast-track eligibility
  GitHub link         | Repo languages, commits, complexity | Practical proficiency
  Academic transcripts| Subject scores (math vs coding)     | Analytics stream fit

Privacy: Resumes encrypted at rest. Only L&D Executives see full parsed profile.
Trainers see skill summary tags. Mavericks view/edit own profile.

11. GAMIFICATION DESIGN — THE NEXTSTEPS GAME LAYER
================================================================================

Gamification is structural, not cosmetic. Every meaningful training action has
an XP value. Without feedback, Mavericks cannot progress. The game layer IS the
feedback completion engine.

XP System — How Mavericks Earn
------------------------------
  Action                              | XP           | Rationale
  ------------------------------------|--------------|----------------------------
  Attend session on time (QR verified)| 50           | Presence must be earned
  Submit Pulse Feedback (30 sec)      | 30           | Lowest friction, highest freq
  Submit Deep Feedback (24hr, 8 Qs)   | 80           | Higher effort = higher reward
  Quiz score 70–79%                   | 60           | Encourages attempting
  Quiz score 80–89%                   | 100          | Strong performance
  Quiz score 90–100%                  | 150 (+50)    | Excellence on leaderboard
  Submit peer feedback                | 40           | Honest peer reflection
  7-day feedback streak               | 200 bonus    | Duolingo-inspired consistency
  AI Learning Buddy question          | 10 (max 30/d)| Prevents gaming
  Upload resume + certificates        | 100 one-time | Activates AI Skill Profile
  Complete phase milestone            | 500          | Major achievement (Ph 1–4)
  Batch hits 90% feedback this week   | 200 (batch)  | Collective peer accountability

  Voice of the Batch | Feedback cited by L&D for curriculum     | Very Rare
  Certified First    | Upload relevant cert before Day 5        | Common
  Top Trainer        | Batch scores above threshold 3 sessions  | Trainer badge

KEY INSIGHT: Feedback IS the game. No feedback = no XP, broken streak, dropped
rank, stalled badges. Batch mechanic: "4 submissions away from 90% — everyone
gets 200 XP."
